About us

We’re a female-owned Johannesburg based music studio made up of 5 creatives with more than a decade experience. We specialise in creating music compositions that attend to your brief and storyline by blending African soul with global precision.

Our passion lies in creating your music that is culturally relevant and functional for your campaign needs.

 
 
 

INDUSTRY FACT

In SA, under 5% of music studios are female-owned (per SAMRO Foundation), with women of colour owning far below 2% due to a lack of technical training, major capital barriers and legacy inequalities.
